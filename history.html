Backgroung of our company

Kihihi Energy Saving Stoves Company Co. Ltd (private) was formed under the sole discretion of Mr. Masiko Lawrence who desired to create a company that producesand sells products and servicesthat conserve both energy and the environment. He started this company in 2018 from Kampala where he discussed this development idea with Tuheisomwe Jackline and Mugisha Denis. They crystallized their thoughts and plans to form a company that serves both Uganda and other countries. They resolved the company to have branches in all districts in Uganda. They now have branches in Kampala, Kanungu and KatabarangaMubendeDistrict

Our Current implementation

We are currently involved in making charcoal, energy saving stoves out of steel, clay and concrete which they sell on retail and wholesale.Having realized that women and children were the most affected in the business of collecting firewood. They would be affected by various difficulties like women being raped and both being bitten by snakes or even being killed by wild animals. .

Achievements

We have fabricated over 430 energy saving stoves (Steel215 and Clay215). The Company has a nursery bed of tree seedlings and a main garden of 1 acre where they have planted.
